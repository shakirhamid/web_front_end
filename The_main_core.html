Discover the ancient Chinese civilization

Chinese culture has roots stretching back thousands of years, forming one of the oldest and richest civilizations in the world. From profound philosophy to refined literature, from exquisite art to advanced science, China has provided an invaluable cultural legacy. Works such as Lao Tzu’s Tao Te Ching, The Analects of Confucius, and Sun Tzu’s The Art of War are not just books, but treasures of wisdom that have influenced the world. Come with us on a journey to explore these timeless works, and show how these ideas can inspire us in our modern lives.

The Tao Te Ching (by Laozi)

The Tao Te Ching is a philosophical text that teaches us how to live in harmony with nature and the universal laws. It is considered the foundation of Taoist philosophy, and offers timeless advice on humility, simplicity, and balance.

The Art of War (by Sun Tzu)

The Art of War is a strategy manual still taught in military academies and business schools. It teaches us how to achieve victory using intelligence and planning rather than raw force.

Confucius's Analects

The Analects of Confucius is a collection of dialogues that reflect Confucius's philosophy of ethics, governance, and human relations. It is considered the foundation of traditional Chinese thought.

dream of the red chamber

"Dream of the Red Cabin" is one of the greatest Chinese novels, offering a profound look at social and cultural life in China during the eighteenth century.
